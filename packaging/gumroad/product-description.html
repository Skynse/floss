Floss is a digital painting app built for artists who want professional tools without a cluttered interface.

Paint with responsive brushes, organize work in layers and folders, and open files you already have (PSD, KRA, PNG, and more). Save layered .floss documents or export when you're done.

What you get

  • Linux: AppImage + Flatpak
  • Windows: portable zip + installer
  • macOS: Apple Silicon + Intel builds
